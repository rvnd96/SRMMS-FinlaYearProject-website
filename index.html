What is this Project ?

01.

This project is designed and developed for the final year module named "Comprehensive Design & Analysis Project" as known as the Research Project of Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT) for the BSc Special (Hons) in Information Technology .

The Target Audience ?

02.

The target audience of this project is "Road Delevelopment Authority" of Sri Lanka (RDA) and also any private road repairing and maintaining company .

Why use SRMMS ?

03.

This project, Smart Road Maintenance Management System (SRMMS), automatically detects any road potholes and estimate the material cost with the given material type, labor cost with the given labor skill level, and the total cost with summation of material, labor and other costs.

Explain me the S R M M S

Road maintenance is an essential task for any Country in order to preserve the roads in its originally constructed condition, to protect adjacent resources and user safety, and provide efficient, convenient travel along the route. Unfortunately, maintenance is improperly performed resulting in eventual failure and ends up with high maintenance cost.

Material cost and Labor cost is taken from a document called the HSR. Even though the ministry tried to automate the systems several times, the projects have not been successful for all these years and Millions of dollars were put into waste.

This system (S R M M S) will collect the data using a Vehicle mounted sensor then identify the defects on the road by training the system to detect them. This component will estimate the cost of the overall road maintenance and repairs using cost estimation algorithms and machine learning techniques based on the materials and labor details as Information Technology plays a substantial role in dealing with challenges in construction projects. The results and findings of this component will provide a guide for repairing and maintaining various road distresses and it will help to make the system more accurate and provide detail-based decisions on a budgeting aspect.
